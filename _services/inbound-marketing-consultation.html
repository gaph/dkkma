What is Inbound Marketing?

Essentially, inbound marketing is the opposite of outbound marketing. Rather than reaching out to your prospects via email, phone or advertisement, you are aiming to get to the point, where prospects reach out to you.

  1. Personalized: Targeted, quality content that answers prospects and customers questions and needs.
  2. Multi-Channel: Reach prospects where they are and want to interact with you.
  3. Timing & Analytics: Publish the right content at the right time and measure the reach.
